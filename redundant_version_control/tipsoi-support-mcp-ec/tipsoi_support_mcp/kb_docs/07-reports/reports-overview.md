# Reports Overview

## How to Access Reports

1. Log in to Tipsoi Admin Panel at **https://hrm.tipsoi.pro/login**
2. From the left sidebar, click **Reports**
3. Select the report type you need

All reports have:
- **Filter** — filter by department, designation, sub-department, shift group, workplace, gender, employment type, line manager, HR manager, and status
- **Search** — search by employee name/ID/RFID
- **Date selection** — specific dates or date ranges using calendar
- **Download** — export to PDF and/or Excel format

---

## Attendance Reports

**Path:** Reports → Attendance Report

Sub-reports available:
- Individual Attendance
- Detailed Attendance
- Department Report
- Monthly Report
- Detailed Monthly Report
- Daily Report
- Pandemic Report (Add-on)

### Individual Attendance Report
Shows day-by-day attendance for one employee.

**How to use:**
1. Go to Reports → Attendance Report → Individual Attendance
2. Search employee by name or ID
3. Select the month from the calendar
4. View status, entry time, exit time, working hours, OT hours
5. Download as PDF or Excel

**Shows:** Working Days, Present, Absent, Leave, Late, Field Visits, Actual Hours, Expected Hours, OT Hours per day

---

### Detailed Attendance Report
Shows all employees across a date range.

**Shows:** Each employee with daily entry/exit times, status, expected vs actual hours

**Download:** Excel only (too wide for PDF)

---

### Department Report
Same as Detailed Attendance but filtered by one or multiple departments.

**Download:** Excel

---

### Monthly Report
Monthly summary for all employees — most commonly used report.

**Shows per employee:**
- Working Days, Present, Absent, Leave, Off Days, Holidays, Late, Approved Lates
- Expected Hours, Working Hours, Difference, Overtime Hours

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

### Detailed Monthly Report
Like Monthly Report but shows each individual day's status in a grid (absent/present/late per date).

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

### Daily Report
Shows attendance status for all employees for today or a specific date.

**Summary shows:** Total Employees, Leave, Late, Day Off, Present, Absent, Early Exit, No Out Punch

**Table shows each employee:** Entry, Exit, Late minutes, Early Exit, Working Hours, OT Hours

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

## Leave Reports

**Path:** Reports → Leave Report

### Leave History Report
Shows all leave taken by employees.

**Filter by:** Employee, Month, Leave Category (Annual Leave, Earned Leave, Medical Leave, Maternity Leave, etc.), Approval Status (Pending/Approved/Rejected)

**Shows:** Total Leave Days, Total Working Hour Loss; employee name, leave category, date range, days, approval status, reason

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

### Leave Balance Report
Shows remaining leave balance for each employee.

**Shows:** Leave Policy, Leave Type, Entitled days, Used days, Remaining Balance

Filter by year. Download: Excel only.

---

## Absent Reports

**Path:** Reports → Absent Report

### Daily Absent Report
Who was absent on a specific date.

**Summary:** Total Employees, Total Absent, Working Hour Loss

**Table:** Employee, Workplace, Shift, Last Absent, Absent in last 30 days, Line Manager

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

### Absent Summary Report
Total absent days across a date range per employee.

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

## Late Reports

**Path:** Reports → Late Report / Daily Late Report

### Late Report
All employees who were late in a date range. Shows date, count, employee name, shift, entry time, how late (minutes).

**Download:** PDF and Excel

### Daily Late Report
Who was late on a specific day. Shows: Entry time, Late by minutes, Application Status (whether employee applied to excuse), Exit time, Working Hours, Total Late this month, Line Manager.

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

## Working Hour Report

**Path:** Reports → Working Hour Report

Shows total hours worked in a date range.

**Per employee:** Expected hours, Actual hours, Difference, Average hours for each day in range.

**Download:** PDF and Excel

---

## Field Visit Report

**Path:** Reports → Field Visit Report

Shows all field visits: Employee name, Date, Reason, Entry/Exit time, First Approver Status, Second Approver Status, Final Status, Applied By.

**Download:** Excel

---

## Mobile Punch Report

**Path:** Reports → Mobile Punch Report

Shows employees who punched via the Tipsoi mobile app with GPS location.

**Shows:** Date, Name, ID, GPS Location address, In time, Out time, Image, Remarks.

**Download:** Excel

---

## Location Tracking Report

**Path:** Reports → Location Tracking Report

Shows GPS location history for employees with location tracking enabled.

---

## Salary Report

**Path:** Reports → Salary Report

Shows salary data and payroll records.

---

## Filter Options (All Reports)

Click **Filter** in any report to narrow results by:
- Department, Sub-Department
- Designation
- Shift Group
- Gender, Employment Type
- Workplace
- Line Manager, HR Manager
- Status: Active, Inactive, Terminated, Resigned, Transferred

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I view the monthly attendance report?**
A: Go to Reports → Attendance Report. Use the calendar to select the full month. Choose Monthly Report from the sub-menu.

**Q: How do I download a report?**
A: After the report loads, click the **Excel** or **PDF** button at the top right.

**Q: How do I filter by department?**
A: Use the Department Report, or use the Filter option in any report.

**Q: How do I see who was absent today?**
A: Go to Reports → Absent Report → Daily Absent Report. It shows today by default.

**Q: Can I see reports for two different offices?**
A: Yes, if Workplaces are set up. Use the Filter → Workplace option to view each office separately.

**Q: How do I find employees who are consistently late?**
A: Use the Late Report with a monthly date range. Sort by late count.
